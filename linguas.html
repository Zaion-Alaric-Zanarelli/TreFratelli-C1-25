Cellar and Restaurant Tre Fratelli

Our story Tre Fratelli has its roots in 1925, when Giovanni decided to cultivate Chiavennasca — the mountain Nebbiolo — on the terraces overlooking Tirano. Through economic crises, war, and the resurgence of alpine tourism, the family has upheld a simple tradition: wine, hospitality, stonework, and patience. In the difficult years, the barrels were protected in a small “secret cellar” carved into the rock; during the recovery years, the grandchildren combined agricultural knowledge with oenological studies, never abandoning the dry-stone walls that hold the mountain. Today, the fourth generation continues the family legacy with the same philosophy: good wine with good food, in a place that smells of wood, bread, and valley. The cellar Small, essential, carved into the stone. Controlled fermentations, aging in oak barrels, and long rest: the mountain demands time, and we respect it. Our wines Classic Line Valpolicella Tre Fratelli 2019 — A lively and convivial red (inspired edition, limited production). Tre Chardonnay 2022 — A fresh white, with apple and mountain flower notes. Merlot Tre Fratelli 2020 — Ripe fruit, hints of cocoa, smooth tannins. *Historic labels of the estate, originally created as style exercises for the restaurant industry Mountain & Tradition Handcrafted productions inspired by the Valtellina character: local grapes, manual harvesting, slow aging. Each vintage tells the story of the climate and the work on the terraces. The restaurant The cuisine is simple and of the valley: pizzoccheri, local cured meats and cheeses, game when in season, and grandmother’s desserts.
